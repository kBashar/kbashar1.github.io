Wings of desire

In actuality Wings of Desire, a movie from 1987, is a poetry. This was my first expression immediately after the 160 minutes long movie. That vindication remains same after two days and another re-watch. This is one of those few times when I really don’t have anything coherent to say about the art I enjoyed so much. It is not long ago that I made peace with the distance I have with poetry, previously ignored, denied, vehemently opposed and now I believe Poetry is here to usher us in the labyrinth that language is. Watching this movie has solidified my conviction on that.
