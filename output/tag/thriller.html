Get Out(2017)

Comedian Jordan Peele’s first feature film “Get out” have an original as well as thrilling screenplay to entice you into a sitting of hundred minutes of comedy, drama and horror.

A social satire movie turns into a horror thriller with every passing frames. Movie title starts in the backdrop of a sun drenched forest fringed by a highway. But not long before crippling silence engulfs the serenity when a deer hits the car carrying a frisky couple.

A Black boyfriend comes, however reluctantly, to his girlfriend’s family country estate to meet her parents. Liberal parents, who would vote for Obama for third time if they could, welcome Chris (the boyfriend) with awkward jokes. I couldn’t stop myself seeing a parallel between the pretentious liberal father and untravelled moderate muslims around us who wear a veil of tolerance only to surface up whenever put on test. While heap of hypocrisies mount on, we also notice some oddities around, so does Chris.

Would I recommend to watch?

Ya, it’s thrilling 100 minutes.
